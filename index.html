Our Story

Kraken Seafood Restaurant was born from a passion for sustainable seafood and modern coastal cuisine. Founded in 2018 by Chef Marcus Hayes, we've established ourselves as the premier destination for seafood lovers seeking fresh, responsibly-sourced ingredients prepared with creativity and care.

Our relationships with local fishermen and purveyors ensure that only the freshest catches make it to your plate. From our waterfront location to our open kitchen, we've created an atmosphere that celebrates the ocean's bounty in every detail.
